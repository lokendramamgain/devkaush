Devkaush organization

DevKaush is a sum of “DEV” which means GOD in Sanskrit & “KAUSH” which means PURITY in Sanskrit. The Purities from the God is the meaning of DEVKAUSH. DevKaush Organization is a group of youth which is working for village empowerment and impeding the rate of migration in Uttarakhand state. Agriculture & Horticulture, Architecture & Sustainable Construction, Garhwal Tourism  based activities are the focus areas as it bestows livelihood and sustainable income options to the community.
A group of reverse migrated youth is dedicated to the hill community and provide them ample opportunity to re-establish themselves by self employment.
We deliver our services to the community by providing services on Agriculture & Food Processing, Architecture & Sustainable Construction, Garhwal Tourism and consultancy.
